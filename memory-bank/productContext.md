## ðŸŽ® Game Mechanics Design

### Bidding Strategy

**Fast Mode:**
- Bid on multiple shirts quickly
- Spread risk across designs
- Maximize chances of winning

**Focused Mode:**
- Concentrate bids on favorite design
- Higher commitment, higher stakes
- Build emotional investment

**Conservative Mode:**
- Bid only on designs user loves
- Preserve credits for best opportunities
- Lower frequency, higher intent

### Psychological Hooks

1. **Variable Rewards** - Never know when you'll win
2. **Progress Indicators** - See shirt approaching 250 bids
3. **Social Proof** - Others are bidding (FOMO)
4. **Loss Aversion** - Already invested credits
5. **Achievement** - Winner status feels earned
