OneShirt.app is a gamified t-shirt bidding platform that combines the addictive swipe mechanics of Tinder with competitive auction dynamics. Users bid credits on AI-generated t-shirt designs, creating an engaging experience where the 250th bidder wins the shirt.

---

## ðŸŽ® Core Concept

### The Game Loop

1. **Browse** - Swipe through AI-generated t-shirt designs
2. **Bid** - Swipe right to place a 1-credit bid
3. **Compete** - Race to be the 250th bidder
4. **Win** - Winner gets the physical t-shirt

### Key Mechanics

- **Starting Credits:** 100 per user
- **Bid Cost:** 1 credit per bid
- **Win Threshold:** 250 bids required
- **Winner:** Player who pushes bid count to 250
